## 🐦 What is YieldCanary?

YieldCanary is an ETF analytics dashboard that cuts through the marketing hype of income-focused ETFs. We show you what the fund companies don't want you to see.

**The Problem:** High-yield ETFs are exploding in popularity. Funds advertise 12%, 20%, even 50%+ yields — but most investors don't realize those payouts are often funded by selling off their own principal. That's not income — it's liquidation with extra steps.

**Our Solution:** YieldCanary analyzes over 100 income ETFs and reveals:

- **Death Clock** — How many years until the fund burns through your principal at current distribution rates
- **True Income Yield** — The real yield after stripping out Return of Capital (ROC)
- **Take-Home Cash Return** — Your actual return after taxes, the number that matters for retirement planning

---

## 💡 Key Metrics Explained

### Death Clock
How long until this ETF's NAV hits zero at current distribution rates. A 2-year death clock means the fund is burning through principal fast.

### True Income Yield
The headline yield minus Return of Capital. If a fund advertises 15% yield but 60% is ROC, your true income yield is only 6%.

### Take-Home Cash Return
Your actual return after accounting for taxes on distributions. This is the number that matters for your bank account.

### ROC % (Return of Capital)
The percentage of distributions that come from your own invested money rather than actual fund earnings. High ROC = paying yourself with your own money.
